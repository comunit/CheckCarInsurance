1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They enable the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and provide certain functionality.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the functioning of the Website and enable you to navigate and use its features. They are usually set in response to your actions, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling forms.

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use the Website, such as which pages are visited most often or if any error messages occur. We use this information to improve the performance and functionality of the Website.

2.3 Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the Website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may also be used to provide certain services you request, such as live chat support.

2.4 Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. They track your browsing activities across different websites and may be set by third-party advertising networks.

4.3 Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising: If you wish to opt-out of targeted advertising based on your browsing activities, you can visit the websites of the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) or the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) for more information and opt-out options.
